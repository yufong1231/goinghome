About

To help you get home by train smoothly.

  • Pick your date

    Check the timetable for that day.

    Enter our app and key in your travel date. We will show you the timetable for that day and help you pick the one which suits you the most.

  • Pick your train

    Choose from the trains we present.

    After choosing which train you would like, add the train to FAVORITES. You will receive every information of your train.

  • Select your Alarm

    Tell us when to remind you.

    After making sure the time your train will arrive, tell us when you would like us to give you a reminder. You can decide the time by typing, or simply select from the times we provide.

  • Warning for delay

    Be the first to know for train delay.

    After you add your train into FAVORITES, we will help keep track of the train. We will send you a message if your train is delayed. You can plan your time better knowing the train schedule ahead.
